Racquets -vintage tennis racquet shop

Collectors tennis racquets from past champions

To play tennis you need a racquet, and the racquet is personal to each and every player. It's their main tool on the court, serving as a teammate of sorts in a primarily individual sport.

Development of such an essential and personal piece of equipment has been no simple task over the years, evolving from wood to metal to modern graphite composite frames.

We have five main categories of racquets, from wood, metal/hybrid, through to oversize, graphite and modern frames from proud manufacturers such as Wilson, Head, Dunlop and Babolat.

Please browse our collection of highly sought after racquets from past champions, many are collectors items and highly sought after.
